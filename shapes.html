Drawing

My passion is drawing/doodling.

I am passionate about drawing because it is really fun to draw shapes, objects and emotions/feelings. In school, I always doodle on the side of pages in notebooks or worksheets, it always spices up a boring paper.


I was introduced to it in elementary, and kept on doodling.

I got my inspiration from Amanda Rach Lee, I started watching her journaling videos beginning High School. Amanda Rach Lee continues to inspire me and I have gotten better at doodling similar or different shapes plus it helps me to stay focused on the job at hand.

Facts about doodling

  • Acts as a tool for creative problem solving
  • Stress relieving
  • Memory recall
  • Mood regulation
  • Creativity and authenticity.
